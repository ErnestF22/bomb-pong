HOW TO PLAY:

You are gonna play DaBombPong!
Be careful with Bomb. If you miss a bomb you will blow up!

Hit the bomb on the right side to make it go left and viceversa.

Watch the bomb timer. If it runs out a bomb will be blown up.
Try to blow it up at opponent side not yours.

First to 5 points wins!

If you want more fun choose fast mode.
